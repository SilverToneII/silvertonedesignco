## Approach

I led a team of `{{ N }}` designers and researchers through three discovery sprints, focusing on:

- **Citizen interviews** (`{{ N }}` sessions across `{{ X }}` states) to understand actual application contexts — not the assumed ones
- **Plain-language content audits** in partnership with the agency's communications team
- **Accessibility-first prototyping** that started with screen reader flow before visual design
